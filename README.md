# VoltEdge-System-Project
I have analyzed VoltEdge Systems, a mid-sized electronics manufacturing company's dataset using Jupyter Notebook and Pandas for data cleaning, while used SQl and it"s various functions like CTE, Window function, JOIN etc for analysing. By analyzing relationship across products, customers and orders and have identified various patterns, inconsistencies and trends of business.

The analysis reveals an industrial automation and IoT products business with distinct pricing patterns - products above the ₹356.9 average price point are premium industrial components like IoT gateways and smart controllers. About 26.5% of customers (396 out of 1,489) are inactive, representing untapped potential for re-engagement strategies.

Total sales reach $22.36 million, with top 10 products contributing $3.21 million (14.3%), dominated by automation controllers and sensor modules. The average order value of $17,428 shows significant variation, with some orders reaching $35,424, indicating substantial enterprise purchases.

Within the Apex Collection, Communication Module leads demand, followed by Charging Controller and Power Supply Unit, highlighting preferences for communication and charging solutions. Customer segmentation reveals that high-value customers drive most revenue, while 23.34 lakh low-value customers present conversion opportunities.

Order analysis shows diverse buying patterns from single-item to bulk purchases, enabling bundling strategies. Chronological tracking of orders helps identify purchase frequency and retention opportunities.
